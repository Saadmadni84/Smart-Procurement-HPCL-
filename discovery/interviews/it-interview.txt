IT / SAP Interview Guide

Purpose: Understand data availability, extract methods, integration constraints, and security.

Questions:
1. What SAP modules and tables store PR/PO/master data for procurement?
2. What formats can you export (CSV, IDoc, OData, API)? Any sample extracts available?
3. Who owns the vendor master and how often is it refreshed?
4. Which fields are authoritative in SAP for price, quantity, GL account, cost center?
5. Are there existing APIs or middleware (PI/PO, CPI) we can use for integration?
6. What SSO / authentication methods are available (SAML, OAuth)?
7. Data retention, anonymization, and masking rules for sharing samples?
8. Any performance constraints for real-time rule evaluation against SAP data?
9. How do you currently process approvals in SAP (workflow engine)?
10. Can you provide a sample mapping between SAP field names and business terms?

Open probes:
- Batch frequency for data syncs
- Sandbox access for dev/testing
- Change control processes for schema changes
