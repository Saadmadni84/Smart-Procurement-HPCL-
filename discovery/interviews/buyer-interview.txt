Buyer Interview Guide

Purpose: Understand how buyers raise PRs, common exceptions, supporting docs, and pain points.

Questions:
1. Describe the end-to-end process you follow to create a PR (step-by-step).
2. Which fields are mandatory in your PR form? What fields are often missing or inaccurate?
3. How do you select vendors? Is there a vendor master or ad-hoc entry?
4. What justifications are typically attached for single-source or emergency purchases?
5. How are estimated values calculated and who validates them?
6. What supporting documents do you attach? (commercial, technical, approvals)
7. How do you track tender activities and vendor queries?
8. Describe frequent approval bottlenecks or rework loops.
9. Which approvals do you find unclear or redundant?
10. Are there existing templates or forms (Excel, SharePoint) you reuse?
11. What data fields would you consider safe to auto-fill from SAP?
12. Which rules would you expect to be automated vs. reviewed manually?
13. What audit trails are required for compliance and who reviews them?
14. Example PRs: please provide 3 anonymized PR IDs and attached docs.

Open probes:
- Times when an urgent procurement bypassed standard approvals
- Instances of vendor disputes or blacklisting
- Any integrations currently used (email alerts, SAP workflows)
