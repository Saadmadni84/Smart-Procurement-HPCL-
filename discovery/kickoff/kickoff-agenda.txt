90-minute Kickoff Agenda — Discovery Phase

Duration: 90 minutes

Attendees (required): Procurement Head, Category Managers, Finance Lead, IT/SAP Lead, Legal/Compliance Rep, Project Sponsor, Product Owner

Timing (minutes) — Owner
-------------------------------------------------
0-5  — Welcome & objectives (Project Sponsor)
5-15 — Introductions & scope (Product Owner)
15-30 — High-level timeline & milestones (Project Manager)
30-45 — Current procurement flow walkthrough (Procurement Head)
45-60 — CVC/PSU compliance highlights (Legal/Compliance)
60-75 — Data & integration requirements (IT/SAP)
75-85 — Q&A and critical risks (All)
85-90 — Immediate next steps & actions (Project Manager)

Required pre-kickoff actions:
- Request participants review `discovery/README.md`
- IT to prepare sample SAP exports (anonymized) for Week 1
- Legal to bring template clauses for single-source approvals
